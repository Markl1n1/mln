Bio profile for Scott Rofey

Scott Rofey

Scott Rofey is the Global Head of Rates, Macro and Commodities Risk and a member of the Office of the CIO. He is responsible for managing all risks within our Rates and Macro businesses.

Prior to joining Millennium, he was a Partner and the head of US Interest Rates Product Trading, with additional responsibilities for Repo and Short Term Macro Trading, and co-head of Global Interest Products Trading at Goldman Sachs. Scott’s trading background is in US Interest Rate Options and Exotics, both at Goldman Sachs which he joined in 2003, and at JPMorgan where he began his career in 1997.

Scott holds a BA in Biology, as well as a Certificate in Teacher Preparation from Princeton University.
