Employee networks

The Women of Millennium Network, Women in Investments Network and Women in Technology Network empower and connect women at the firm through opportunities for networking, professional development and mentoring.

MLNride cultivates an inclusive workplace for LGBTQ+ employees, raising visibility and awareness and giving back to the community.

The Millennium Multicultural Network promotes inclusivity among all employees while increasing awareness and understanding of issues relating to race, culture and ethnicity.

The Millennium Volunteer Network provides opportunities for employees to collaborate with one another while directly giving back to the community.

The Inclusion Network encourages the development of a more inclusive work environment through a variety of events, discussion groups, guest speakers and networking opportunities.

The Families at Millennium Network supports employees through all stages of caregiving by creating a culture of balance between work and family life.
