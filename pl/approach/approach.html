Investment strategies

Millennium seeks to pursue a diverse range of investment strategies across industry sectors, asset classes and geographies.

Fundamental Equity

Conduct fundamental research on companies both as generalists and as specialists within a particular sector or sub-sector.

Equity Arbitrage

Pursue a range of systematic and fundamental arbitrage strategies across a company’s capital structure and a variety of derivatives.

Fixed Income

Manage various strategies such as rates, macro, credit, mortgages and asset-backed securities.

Commodities

Deploy a range of investment strategies across energy, metals, agricultural and soft commodities markets.

Quantitative Strategies

Build and enhance investment processes that are primarily systematically driven and focused on a variety of asset classes.

Rigorous approach to compliance

We are focused on effectively navigating our dynamic and multi-jurisdictional regulatory environment. Our professional in-house legal and compliance teams provide control, guidance and oversight.

Nearly a decade ago, we established a Regulatory and Compliance Advisory Council comprised of external industry experts. The council helps us gain a more comprehensive perspective on critical regulatory developments and adapt our policies and procedures in accordance with best practices.
